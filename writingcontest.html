ABOUT THE WRITING CONTEST

Who is Bytes & Pieces?

  • We are a student-run/youth organization that hosts events in the topics of coding and music and is the organizer/host of this writing contest:).

What is our Mission?

  • Our mission is to increase participation, diversity, and inclusion in the fields of coding and music among youth and adults alike, but specifically focusing on students in middle school, high school, and college.

Writing Contest Overview

  • Tech or Music Writing Contest is a topic article writing contest for teens and adults. Participants can either choose to write about a technology topic or a music topic to win awards. More details about our contest is at this website and Google Doc linked at the next section of this website.

Who Can Attend?

  • Anyone 13 or older. There are two age categories, one ages 13-18 and one 18+.
